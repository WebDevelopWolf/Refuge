Follow a newly formed family as they deal with a pre and post apocolyptic Great Britain that has been shaped and changed by the COVID-19 pandemic of 2020.

Explore the characters past, see how they met and the trials they endured in normal average pre-apoclyptic life to shape and determine their future together. However, rememeber that every decision, statement and action has a consequence, whether that be positive or negative.

In 2019, two young women find themselves, love and each other in the most bleak of circumstances in a domestic abuse refuge in Walsall. Will they to find to the love they both deserve and want from each other and regardless of this option will they choose to be open and honest about their relationship or start their journey behind closed doors for the sake of the children, the community or themselves.

Then later in 2020 when the unthinkable happens and the world is hit with a pandemic that effects people of ages, races and class, how will they survive the most turbulent aspect of their relationship so far? And will the bonds formed in the early days of their life in the Refuge be a help or a hinderance? Most importantly what will the relationships of the people around them teach the children trapped with them? And can all seven family members make it through 2020 alive?

This game, although just a simple multiple-choice adventure, makes use of real-life and edited footage, cleverly designed audio and engaging plot lines to tell the story of two damaged lovers fight for survivial in a pre and post apocolyptic world.

We strongly advise you play the game with headphones capible of outputting 3D THX sound to enhance your Refuge experience and for full immersion into Liane and Kirsty's world.
